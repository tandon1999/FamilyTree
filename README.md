# TandanFamily
A Family Tree application is a digital platform designed to help families organize, preserve, and share their lineage and history. It goes beyond a simple visual representation of familial connections, offering a comprehensive suite of features to enrich the user's understanding of their roots.   

**Core Functionality and Features:**

**Home with Navigation:**
Serves as the central hub, providing easy access to all sections of the application through a clear and intuitive navigation menu.
May feature a welcome message, recent updates, or featured family members.

**Family Tree:**
The core feature, allowing users to build and visualize their family tree.   
Supports adding individuals, defining relationships (parents, siblings, spouses, children), and displaying the tree in various formats (e.g., hierarchical, radial).   
Includes detailed profiles for each family member, containing personal information, photos, and life events.

**Family Timeline:**
A chronological representation of significant family events, such as births, marriages, deaths, and other milestones.   
Provides a historical context for the family's journey, making it easier to understand generational changes.

**Family Dictionary:**
A repository of family-specific terms, nicknames, inside jokes, and other unique expressions.
Helps preserve the family's cultural identity and language.   

**Galleries:**
A multimedia section for storing and displaying family photos, videos, and audio recordings.   
Allows users to create albums, tag family members, and add descriptions to media files.

**Blogs:**
A platform for family members to share stories, anecdotes, and reflections on their experiences.
Fosters communication and connection across generations.   

**Admin Login:**
Provides secure access to administrative functions for managing user accounts, content, and application settings.

**Family Setup:**
Allows Administrators to manage the core family tree data, and add, edit, or remove members of the family.

**Blogs Setup:**
Allows Administrators to manage the blogs, and create, edit, or remove blog posts.

**Galleries Setup:**
Allows administrators to manage the galleries, and add, edit, or remove photos and videos.

**History:**
A section dedicated to preserving historical documents, records, and stories related to the family.
May include scanned documents, transcribed interviews, or research findings.   

**Dashboard:**
Provides an overview of the family tree's data, such as the number of family members, recent activity, and key statistics.
Offers insights into the family's history and growth.   

**Enhanced User Profile:**
Beyond the basic name and photo, a robust User Profile should include:
Personal Information:
Full Name (with options for preferred names/nicknames)
Date of Birth (with privacy settings for year/age display)
Place of Birth
Gender/Pronouns
Current Location
Contact Information (email, phone, social media links - with privacy options)
Occupation/Education
Marital Status
Religious/Cultural Affiliations


**Overall Purpose:**

The Family Tree application serves as a central repository for family history, fostering a sense of connection and shared identity. It aims to:
Preserve family history for future generations.
Facilitate communication and connection among family members.
Provide a platform for sharing stories, memories, and cultural traditions.   
Offer a user-friendly and engaging experience for exploring one's roots.
